Saving Money Machine, Inc.

A 3-Step Plan

Step 1: Cut the Fat

Every week, every low-level employee are required to write a 500-word paper on the value of their contributions

  • If they are not willing to do this, they are unmotivated — fired
  • If they do not submit at least 100 words over the word count, they are doing the bare minimum — fired
  • Submissions must incorporate the blockchain
The merit of each submission will be decided via generative AI to avoid overhead ($$$)

Step 2: Nominal Raises

At this point, some employees may be unmotivated due to unavoidable economic circumstances (not due to our actions). To retain our remaining workforce, all employees will receive a $10.00 raise.

Step 3: Save the Investors

Investors are pulling funding due to our "regressive" climate change policies. Here is the plan:

  1. We announce an ambitious goal to reduce our emissions. The estimated date is far in the future.
  2. We do not work towards this goal
  3. News articles are written about Money Machine's ($$$) ambitious, forward-thinking goals.
  4. Investors give our money ($$$) back to us
